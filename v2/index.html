ABOUT
We started Florin Software because we saw a common pattern in the industry with the shift from legacy monolithic code bases, with little to no testing to a stronger focus on modular smaller services with a focus on service separation, infrastructure and quality. We started as a family there has always been a strong desire in the Florin family in general to steer their own ship. From the Florin Dairy in Winona to a wood working shop in Florida.
