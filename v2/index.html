About

We started Florin Software because we saw a common pattern in the industry with the shift from legacy monolithic code bases, with little to no testing to a stronger focus on modular smaller services and a focus on service separation, infrastructure and quality. This also introduces a cultural change in development teams, one where collaboration and communication becomes much more important, and a focus point for development teams.

Additionally at Florin Software Consulting we employee several full cycle developers who have worked in all parts of the software development process, applying their development expertise to that part of the process. Whether it is automating tests through gherkin and cucumber, deploying to AWS with terraform, writing Go or NodeJS microservices, they or writing large React or Angular frontends.
